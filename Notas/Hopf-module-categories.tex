This note gathers properties of categories of Hopf modules.

\paragraph
Fix a commutative ring $\k$ and a Hopf algebra $H$ over $\k$. We assume throughout that
$H$ is flat over $\k$. We denote by $\Mod^H$ the category of right $H$-comodules, and
${}^H \Mod$ the category of left $H$-comodules. We also fix an $H$-comodule algebra $A$,
and denote by $\Mod^H A$ the category of right $(H,A)$-Hopf modules, that is, its objects
are simultaneously $A$-modules, $H$-comodules, and these structures are compatible in the
obvious sense. Morphisms in $\Mod^H A$ are both $A$-linear and $H$-colinear.

\paragraph
\textbf{The category $\Mod^H$.}
We first list some properties of the category of $H$-comodules. For proofs see
\cite{BW}*{3.13}
\begin{enumerate}
	\item It has direct sums, kernels and cokernels.
	\item It is a Grothendieck category.
	\item The forgetful functor $\Mod^H \to \Mod \k$ has a left adjoint, namely $- \ot_\k
		H$.
\end{enumerate}

\paragraph
\textbf{The category $\Mod^H A$.}
Notice that $\Mod^H A$ has direct sums, kernels and cokernels, and that the forgetful
functor $\Mod^H A \to \Mod \k$ commutes with them.
